Streamlined Fleet Condition Management

Managing a vehicle fleet is challenging. PAVE removes complexity allowing you to know the condition of a fleet vehicle anytime, anywhere, automatically.

Automate vehicle inspections and save fleet managers time and money with PAVE:

  • Vehicle Onboarding - Real time insight into the condition of a vehicle for in-fleeting
  • Vehicle Offboarding - Real time insight into the condition of a vehicle for de-fleeting and disposal
  • Fleet Monitoring - Monitor road worthiness and adherence to brand standards
  • Fleet Reconditioning - Early diagnosis of maintenance issues for faster vehicle routing
  • Daily Vehicle Inspection - Turn drivers into trusted inspectors for your daily vehicle inspection checks

Transparent Used Vehicle Retailing

Trust and transparency in the condition of online used vehicle listings.

PAVE allows our clients to automatically disclose minor defects in online used vehicle listings, providing shoppers with transparency, while reducing deal abandonment due to misrepresentation of minor defects on used vehicles. 

Provide Listing Transparency with PAVE:

  • Traditional Used Vehicle Retailing – Utilize existing retail imagery to automatically disclose minor defects in used vehicle listings to increase trust and transparency in online vehicle listings

  • 360 Used Vehicle Retailing – Utilize existing 360 spin provider imaging to automatically disclose minor defects with in-image damage disclosures with PAVE® Hotspots
